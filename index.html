Crafts for Care

Healthy Crafting, Loving Donation

Who Are We?

Crafts for Care is an organization devoted to promoting mental health and spreading love in the community through art and crafting.

Why Do What We Do?

Art can't cure cancer, it can't end world hunger, it can't fix a broken relationship. But it can be a small glimmer of hope for someone in any of those situations. That's what Crafts for Care is about - being the lone star in the sky for those struggling with mental health, the one there for the entire community.
